Learn & Pop Chinese Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 1, 2025

Welcome to "Learn & Pop Chinese"! We are committed to respecting and protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information when you use our App.

1. Information Collection

1.1 No Personal Identifiable Information (PII) Collection

"Learn & Pop Chinese" is an App that provides all its features without requiring login. Therefore, we do not collect, store, or otherwise process any personally identifiable information that could be used to identify you, including but not limited to your name, email address, phone number, Google account ID, or precise geolocation.

1.2 Automatically Collected Non-Personal Information

We may automatically collect non-personal information to maintain and improve the App's performance and stability:

  • Device Information: Such as device model and operating system version.
  • Usage Data: For analyzing App crashes and error reporting (for example, using Firebase Crashlytics or similar non-personally identifiable tools). This data is anonymous and cannot be linked to specific users.

2. Use of Information

We only use the automatically collected non-personal information for the following purposes:

  • Service Maintenance and Improvement: Diagnosing and fixing App errors and technical issues, ensuring stable operation across different devices.
  • Performance Analysis: Understanding usage trends of App features to optimize user experience.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

Since we do not collect personally identifiable information:

  • We do not sell, rent, or share any information that could identify you personally.
  • Service Providers: Automatically collected non-personal information may be shared with our technical service providers (such as analytics and error reporting services). This information is used only in aggregated and anonymous form to help us maintain the App.

4. Data Security

We have implemented reasonable measures to protect the non-personal data on your device. Since we do not process your personally identifiable information, there is no risk of sensitive information leakage.

5. User Rights and Choices

  • You can use all features of this App without providing any personal information.
  • Opt-out: You can stop data collection at any time by uninstalling the App.

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any significant changes will be published on this page.
